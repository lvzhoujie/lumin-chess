Privacy Policy for Lumin Chess

Effective date: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lumi Industries ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the Lumin Chess mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

Information We Collect

Lumin Chess is designed to operate fully offline. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from your device. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation, sign-in, or any user credentials.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, contact lists, location data, photos, files, or any other personal data.
  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not transmit any data over the network from the App.

Game state, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only and are not synchronized to any external server.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App does not collect data of any kind, no special children's data handling is required.

Permissions

The App does not request runtime permissions for camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, or any other sensitive resource.

Third Parties

The App does not integrate with third-party services, ad networks, social networks, or analytics providers. Your gameplay is not shared with anyone.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is nothing to retain or delete from our systems. If you wish to clear local game state, uninstall the App from your device.

Security

Since no personal data leaves your device, the App is not subject to data-breach risk on our side. Standard mobile operating-system protections (sandboxing, file isolation) apply to data stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices, contact us at support@peterlyu.com.
