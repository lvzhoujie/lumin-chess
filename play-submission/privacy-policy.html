Lumin Chess — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Lumin Chess is published by Lumi Industries. This page explains exactly what data the app collects, stores, shares, or transmits.

Summary

Lumin Chess collects no personal data, sends no data over the network, and uses no analytics, ads, or trackers. Everything runs locally on your device.

What the app stores on your device

Lumin Chess uses your device's local storage (the browser's localStorage) to remember:

  • Which level you're on
  • Your personal best move count for each level
  • Whether you've completed the tutorial
  • Which levels you've unlocked

None of this data ever leaves your device. There is no cloud sync, no account system, and no telemetry. Uninstalling the app removes all of it.

What the app does not do

  • It does not collect or transmit any personal information.
  • It does not contain any advertising or third-party SDKs.
  • It does not use analytics services.
  • It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos, or any other sensitive permission.
  • It does not make any network requests.

Permissions requested

The Android package requests one permission: INTERNET. This is a default Capacitor / WebView requirement and is not actually used to send or receive any data. Lumin Chess never makes a network call.

Children

Lumin Chess is suitable for all ages and does not direct content at children under 13 specifically. Because the app collects no personal data, it is COPPA-compliant by design.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what data the app collects or stores, we'll update this page and bump the "Last updated" date above.
