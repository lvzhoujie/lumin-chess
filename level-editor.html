Storage

Levels auto-save to your browser. Use Download to write levels.json + levels.md to your Downloads folder.
Click cells with the selected tool. Right-click clears. All cells start active — toggle off the ones you want excluded.

Tools

Tap Toggle active to remove a cell from the playable shape (it goes dark). All other cells stay active by default.
The goal state is auto-derived: every W must become B and every B must become W. Gray pieces stay put.
